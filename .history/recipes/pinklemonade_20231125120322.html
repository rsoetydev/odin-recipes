Lasagna Recipe

Description

An easy pink lemonade recipe that saves me lots of time. This drink satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

Ingredients

  • 9 cups water
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 cups fresh lemon juice
  • 1 cup cranberry juice, chilled
  • Ice as needed

Directions

  1. Combine water, sugar, lemon juice, and cranberry juice in a large pitcher; stir until sugar dissolves. Serve in tall glasses over ice.
